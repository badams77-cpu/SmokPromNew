Vapid Promotions

Vapid Promotions is a platform to promote your business over Social media, by each day search for relevant Media Posts and Replying with your advertisement.

We provide a daily tweeter search for tweeter containing your search terms, and then mail a custom reply and custom direct message to the tweets senders.

We are compliant with UK laws, including the trade descriptions act, so your advertisements must at least partially do as described.
